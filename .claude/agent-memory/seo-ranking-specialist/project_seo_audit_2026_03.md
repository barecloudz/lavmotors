Full SEO audit completed 2026-03-26. Key findings:

**Current Indexation:** Google has indexed ~3 pages from the NEW site (homepage + 2 old Wix blog posts still showing). The new service pages, about, contact, and blog pages ARE in the sitemap but may not yet be fully indexed.

**Critical Issue - Hours Inconsistency:** Business hours differ across pages:
- Schema/Footer/Hero/Contact: Mon-Sat 9AM-7PM, Sun 12PM-7PM
- CTA component: Mon-Fri 8AM-7PM, Sat 8AM-5PM
- About page: Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Sun Closed
This NAP inconsistency is an SEO and trust killer. Must resolve with the actual hours.

**Top Competitors for "auto repair Hendersonville NC":**
1. Church Street Automotive (autorepairhendersonvillenc.com) - EMD domain, 50+ yrs, full LocalBusiness schema
2. General Auto Repair and Tire (generalautorepairandtire.com) - strong title tag, coupons, "voted best 2023", extensive service pages
3. Kanuga Tire & Auto (kanugatire.com) - WP/Elementor, testimonial carousel
4. North Main Automotive (northmainautomotive.com) - Google Reviews widget
5. K&S Auto Repair (kandsautonc.com) - ASE certified messaging prominent

**Missing Services:** Detailing service page not in services-data.ts despite being mentioned in meta description and memory. Coolant flush and consignment pages also missing.

**Keyword Gaps:** Competitors rank for service-specific + location keywords like "wheel alignment Hendersonville NC", "brake repair Hendersonville NC". LAV Motors has service pages but page titles use just the service name without location qualifier.

**Why:** Understanding the competitive landscape and current SEO state is essential for prioritizing improvements.
**How to apply:** Use this as baseline for measuring SEO progress. Address critical issues first (hours, titles, missing pages).
